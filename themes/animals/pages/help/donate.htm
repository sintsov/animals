In order to assure that Animal Shelter's name, image, and reputation are properly represented, we ask that all third-party fundraisers abide by our event policies. Thank you again for your efforts.

Расчетные счета
  • The event must be promoted in a manner to avoid statements or the appearance of Animal Shelter endorsing any product, firm, organization, individual, or service.
  • The name and logo must be used in accordance with Animal Shelter's graphic standards. All promotional materials must clearly state the percentage of proceeds that will benefit Animal Shelter.
  • Animal Shelter should receive a list of targeted sponsors for the event before they are approached to minimize overlap with other events and/or fundraising campaigns that may be underway, if applicable.
  • Event organizers must obtain their own liability insurance to cover the event. We are not financially liable for the promotion and/or staging of third-party events.
