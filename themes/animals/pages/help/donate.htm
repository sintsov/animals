Финансовая помощь

Онлайн помощь

There is a variety of events you can host to raise money for our shelter. We believe the initial part is the most important one, so why not start from creating your personal fundraising page on Facebook? It’s easy! Once you've set your goal and completed your page, send supporters to this page for easy online giving. Facebook Fundraisers are great for people who wish to have gifts directed to Animal Shelter.

Интернет банкинг

In order to assure that Animal Shelter's name, image, and reputation are properly represented, we ask that all third-party fundraisers abide by our event policies. Thank you again for your efforts.

Расчетные счета
  • The event must be promoted in a manner to avoid statements or the appearance of Animal Shelter endorsing any product, firm, organization, individual, or service.
  • The name and logo must be used in accordance with Animal Shelter's graphic standards. All promotional materials must clearly state the percentage of proceeds that will benefit Animal Shelter.
  • Animal Shelter should receive a list of targeted sponsors for the event before they are approached to minimize overlap with other events and/or fundraising campaigns that may be underway, if applicable.
  • Event organizers must obtain their own liability insurance to cover the event. We are not financially liable for the promotion and/or staging of third-party events.

Gift donations help our shelter continue its message of kindness, compassion, and concern for all living creatures around the world. That is definitely something to celebrate!

You can make a difference in the lives of homeless and neglected animals in our community. We're committed to helping these animals become healthy and happy and to finding them new, loving families.

Animal Shelter receives no state or federal funding. As a nonprofit organization, we rely on the support of the community to make this work possible.

Your donation will help animals in need. Please give generously. Thank you.
